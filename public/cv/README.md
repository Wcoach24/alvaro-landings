# Drop `cv_en.pdf` here

The `Download CV` button on every landing serves `/cv/cv_en.pdf`.

To add the PDF: open https://github.com/Wcoach24/alvaro-landings/upload/main/public/cv
and drag `CV_EN.pdf` from your Desktop. Rename to lowercase `cv_en.pdf` before commit.

Vercel auto-deploys within ~60s after the commit.
